## Options
A) **The "Input Refiner"**
   - **Concept**: A tool that transforms messy notes/audio into structured assets.

B) **The "Niche Research Bot"**
   - **Concept**: An automated agent that scans specific sources to compile unique trends/swipes.
   - **Why**: It reduces noise. It creates a data asset that doesn't exist elsewhere.

C) **The "Personal CRM Agent"**
   - **Concept**: Automated relationship maintenance and follow-up drafting.
   - **Why**: High pain, but high "trust barrier" and potential for "bloat".

## Recommendation
**Option B) The "Niche Research Bot"**

**Why**: 
I am rejecting Option A. **Do not build the Input Refiner.** It will feel magical for one week and useless the next when model capabilities improve.
Option B is the superior "Small Product". It sells a clear outcome: "Stop Doomscrolling, Get the Signal." It leverages automation to do work the human *cannot* do (scanning 1000s of posts), rather than work the human *won't* do (editing notes). It is defensible, sellable, and high-leverage.
